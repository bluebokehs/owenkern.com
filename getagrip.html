Project Details

Background

My friend recently introduced me to rock climbing. Upon finding this pass-time, I realized that I didn't recall of any popular games based around rock climbing. So, during quarantine, I used my time to develop a rock climbing game called Get A Grip!

Development

Over the course of 6 months of development, involving learning data structures and creating all of the art and design, I completed the development of my app!

Completion

Upon completion of Get A Grip, I figured out how to publish it to the iOS App Store. Although I only have under 200 downloads today, I realize that the project taught me many things about development that I never would have learned without taking up this side project during quarantine.
