EZPT

Project Overview

I worked as the Head of User Experience at a start up called EZPT. This role involved creating the designs for the app, building and designing their website, creating advertisements for their social media pages.

Role

Head of User Experience

Project

UX/UI Design

The Story

EZPT is an app that allows users to get real time audio visual feedback while working out using computer vision. EZPT required a seamless user flow that walk users through their exercises without interfering. Using a simple and clean UI, the user is taken through onboarding through finishing a workout seamlessly.

Get A Grip!

Project Overview

I published a game to the App Store using Unity and C#. My friend introduced me to rock climbing, and upon finding this pass-time, I realized that I didn't recall of any popular games based around rock climbing. So, during quarantine, I used my time to develop a rock climbing game.

Role

Creator

Project

Game Dev & UX/UI

The Story

Over the course of 6 months of development, involving learning data structures and creating all of the art and design, I completed the development of my app! Upon completion of Get A Grip, I figured out how to publish it to the iOS App Store. Although I only have under 200 downloads today, I realize that the project taught me many things about development that I never would have learned without taking up this side project during quarantine.
